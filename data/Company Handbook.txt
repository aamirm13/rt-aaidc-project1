Company Handbook (Example)

Work Hours
- Core hours: 10:00–16:00 (local time), Monday to Friday.
- Flexible start: employees may start between 08:00–10:00.

Remote Work
- Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week.
- Employees must be onsite Tuesdays and Thursdays unless approved by their manager.

Leave Policy
- Annual leave: 20 days per year.
- Sick leave: 10 days per year.
- Carryover: up to 5 annual leave days can carry over into the next calendar year.

Expenses
- Meals: reimbursed up to $25/day while traveling.
- Taxis/rideshare: allowed after 21:00 for safety.
- Receipts required for all expenses over $10.

Security
- Do not paste confidential data into public tools.
- Report suspected phishing immediately to IT.
