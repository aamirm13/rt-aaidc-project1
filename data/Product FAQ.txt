Product FAQ

Product: SkyNotes v2.1

Pricing
- Starter: $9/month (up to 3 projects)
- Pro: $19/month (unlimited projects, priority support)

Integrations
- Slack integration available on Pro plan only.
- Google Drive integration available on Starter and Pro.

Data Retention
- Deleted notes are recoverable for 14 days.
- After 14 days, deleted notes are permanently removed.

Support
- Support hours: 09:00–17:00 UTC, Monday–Friday.
- Average first response time: Pro = 6 hours, Starter = 24 hours.
